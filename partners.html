Partnerships/Collaborations established

NCI Kenya as outlined has collaborated with the following:

  • United States National Cancer Institute: collaboration focused on stakeholder coordination and cancer research
  • Getrude’s Children Hospital: collaborating on a 2-year childhood cancer awareness project to train health workers across 9 county referral hospitals on early detection and diagnosis of childhood cancers.
  • American Cancer Society: project to improve chemotherapy handling and administration
  • National Research Fund- working on a scoping review of cancer research conducted over the last 10 years to inform on research priorities
    • Kenya Childhood Cancer Trust: project focused on establishment of a childhood cancer registry
  • Cancer-related conferences: Breast Cancer Symposium held in April 2018 with Aga Khan University Hospital; Kenya International Cancer Conference held in November 2018 in partnership with the Kenya Society of Hematology and Oncology
  • AMREF Health Africa and Cancer Alliance: to develop a curriculum for primary health care providers on early cancer detection and appropriate referral.
